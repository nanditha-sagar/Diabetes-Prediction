Tips to stay healthy & prevent diabetes

  • Move every day — 30 minutes of brisk walking lowers blood sugar and improves insulin sensitivity.
  • Choose whole grains — Replace white rice/maida with brown rice, jowar, bajra, or ragi to slow glucose absorption.
  • Load up on vegetables — Fill half your plate with non-starchy veggies like karela, methi, spinach, and bottle gourd.
  • Limit sugar & sweets — Avoid sweetened drinks, maida-based snacks, and deep-fried foods.
  • Stay hydrated — Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily. Replace tea/coffee with herbal options.
  • Sleep well — Poor sleep disrupts insulin regulation. Aim for 7–8 hours.
  • Manage stress — Cortisol raises blood glucose. Practice yoga, pranayama, or meditation daily.
  • Regular check-ups — Get fasting blood glucose checked every 6 months if you have a family history.

Indian home remedies to control sugar levels

These traditional remedies have been used for centuries and show promising results — always use alongside prescribed treatment.
🌿
Methi (Fenugreek) seeds
Soak 1 tsp of seeds overnight. Drink the water on an empty stomach. Slows sugar absorption and improves insulin sensitivity.
🟢
Karela (Bitter gourd) juice
Drink 50–100 ml fresh juice on an empty stomach. Rich in charantin — mimics insulin and lowers blood glucose naturally.
🌱
Amla (Indian gooseberry)
Drink 1–2 tsp amla juice or eat raw amla daily. High in chromium — regulates carbohydrate metabolism and boosts insulin action.
🍃
Tulsi (Holy basil) leaves
Chew 5–6 fresh tulsi leaves on an empty stomach. Lowers blood sugar and reduces oxidative stress associated with diabetes.
🟡
Haldi (Turmeric) milk
Add ½ tsp turmeric to warm milk before bed. Curcumin reduces inflammation and may improve insulin sensitivity.
🌰
Jamun (Black plum) seeds
Dry and powder jamun seeds. Take ½ tsp with water twice daily. Reduces starch conversion to sugar in the gut.
🧄
Raw garlic (Lehsun)
Eat 2 raw garlic cloves on an empty stomach. Allicin in garlic stimulates insulin secretion and reduces blood glucose.
